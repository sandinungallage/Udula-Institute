FAQ

Common Questions

Everything parents and students ask before enrolling

When can I enroll for classes?
+
Enrollment is open throughout the year. New batches start at the beginning of each term. You can visit the institute between 9:00 AM – 7:00 PM on any weekday, or call us to confirm seat availability.
What is the medium of instruction?
+
Most classes are conducted in Sinhala medium. English medium sessions are available for selected subjects such as English Language and ICT. Please check the timetable for specific medium details.
Are model papers included in the fee?
+
Monthly paper fees (Rs. 100–200 per subject) are charged separately on top of the tuition fee. These cover the cost of printed model papers, past papers, and study sheets provided each month.
Do you offer revision classes before exams?
+
Yes. Special intensive revision sessions are held in the months leading up to both A/L and O/L examinations. These sessions include rapid content review, past paper analysis, and exam technique workshops.
What happens if I miss a class?
+
Students are responsible for keeping up with missed content. Notes and summary sheets are available at the institute's library. For repeated absences, please contact the respective teacher or institute office.
