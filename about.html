Our Story

A Decade of Academic Excellence in Horana

Founded in 2014 by a group of passionate educators, Udula Institute began with a simple mission: to provide high-quality, affordable tuition to the students of Horana and surrounding communities in the Kalutara District.

Over the past decade, we have grown from a small two-room institute to a fully-equipped educational centre with six dedicated teaching halls, a library, and a computer laboratory. Today, over 500 students trust Udula Institute to prepare them for their A/L and O/L examinations.

Our teachers are not just qualified — they are dedicated mentors who go beyond the syllabus to ensure each student understands the subject deeply and develops strong exam technique.

Milestones

Our Journey

2014
Institute Founded
Udula Institute opened its doors with 3 teachers and 45 students, offering O/L Maths and Science classes.
2016
A/L Classes Introduced
Expanded to include A/L Physical Science stream with Combined Maths, Physics, and Chemistry.
2018
New Building & Library
Moved to a larger premises. Opened the student library with past papers archive and reference collection.
2020
Computer Lab Opened
Launched a 25-seat computer laboratory to support ICT practical sessions and digital learning.
2022
Commerce Stream Added
Introduced A/L Commerce stream with Accounting and Economics, completing our subject offerings.
2025
500+ Student Milestone
Reached over 500 enrolled students and celebrated our first island rankers in A/L Combined Mathematics.
